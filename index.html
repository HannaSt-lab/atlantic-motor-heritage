Our Philosophy

Carefully Selected.
Authentically Presented.

Atlantic Motor Heritage was founded with a simple idea: exceptional automobiles deserve careful preservation and knowledgeable ownership.

We specialise in classic sports cars and collector vehicles that represent milestones in European automotive history — from iconic air-cooled Porsche sports cars to rare performance models, each chosen for its authenticity and mechanical character.

Based in Portugal, we serve collectors and enthusiasts across Germany, the Netherlands, Poland, and Europe.
