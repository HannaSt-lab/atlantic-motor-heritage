01 / The Problem

Manual.
Slow.
Invisible.

A European collector car dealer with serious inventory and serious buyers — but every lead that filled out the contact form disappeared into an inbox. No tracking, no follow-up, no record of who enquired about what.

  • No lead visibility

    Inquiries arrived by email with no status, no source, no pipeline. High-value leads were going cold.

  • Zero automation

    Every piece of data entered manually. No CRM, no deal tracking, no record of car interest per contact.

  • No always-on presence

    A buyer browsing at midnight in Germany had no way to get instant answers — and no reason to stay.

05 / The Journey

What happens when a client hits "Send"

00:00.000
Form submitted
The prospect enters their name, email, and the car they're interested in. They hit Send. Nothing visible changes — but the pipeline is already running.
00:00.180
n8n webhook fires
The form data hits the n8n webhook in under 200ms. The workflow validates and routes every field to every connected system simultaneously.
00:00.900
HubSpot contact created
A full contact record lands in HubSpot — name, email, phone, car interest, and a timestamped inquiry note. A deal opens at "New Lead" stage in the pipeline.
00:01.400
Owner notified
A branded Gmail notification arrives with the full lead summary, the specific car they enquired about, and a direct link to the HubSpot record. Response time: under 1.5 seconds.
Ongoing
AI concierge handles the conversation
If the client messages the Telegram bot, the Claude-powered concierge responds instantly — in AMH brand voice, with real product knowledge — 24 hours a day. High-intent buyers are escalated to the owner immediately, with full context.
