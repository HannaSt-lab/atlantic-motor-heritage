## What the Business Is

Atlantic Motor Heritage **buys and sells European collector cars.**
- Based in Portugal
- Buys carefully selected classic and collector cars
- Sells to buyers across Europe (Germany, Netherlands, Poland and beyond)
- Handles export documentation, transport, remote inspections, video walkarounds
- Small curated inventory — quality over quantity
- Feel: private gallery, not a dealership floor

## Target Audience

High-income male collectors from Germany, Netherlands, Poland.
Age 40–65. Seeking air-cooled Porsches and European classics through a trusted, personal source.
